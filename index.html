The Analytical Standard

The Invisible Toxins In Your Cup

Because ceremonial matcha involves consuming the ground entire leaf—not just steeped water—any environmental toxins in the soil end up directly in your system. We reject the lax testing of generic brands.

High-Altitude Mountain Irrigation

Our volcanic-granite soils in Wazuka, Kyoto drain rapidly and prevent the extended water-logging that forces plants to draw heavy metals into their leaves.

ISO-Accredited ICP-MS Testing

Every harvest is pulverized and digested in concentrated acid, then analyzed using Inductively Coupled Plasma Mass Spectrometry. We detect levels down to parts-per-trillion.
