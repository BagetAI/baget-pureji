Natural Volcanic Barriers

Soil geology is our first layer of defense. In standard Japanese tea growing, lowland river basins accumulate heavy metals from historical industrialization. Because tea plants absorb up to 100% of minerals from soil, this contamination directly impacts the dry ground matcha leaf.

Pureji eliminates this risk. We source exclusively from isolated high-altitude micro-lots managed by Obubu Tea Farm and Hori Family Farm in Wazuka, Kyoto.

At heights up to 500m, these farms feature weathered volcanic ash and gravelly granite soils. The rapid natural water drainage prevents long-term root retention, restricting heavy-metal absorption. This is a natural geographical moat.
